Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20/09/2025

Last Updated: 05/01/2026

DictX (“the App”, “we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

01

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require account registration and do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

We do not use advertising identifiers, tracking technologies, or third-party advertising services.

02

User-Provided Content

When you use AI-powered features such as translation, explanations, example generation, or sentence analysis, you may voluntarily provide text content (words, sentences, or notes).

This content is used solely to provide the requested functionality, processed temporarily, and is not stored, linked to your identity, or used for tracking or advertising purposes.

03

AI & Third-Party Processing

To provide AI features, the App may send user-provided text to trusted third-party AI services for real-time processing.

We do not retain this data, build user profiles, or use it for marketing or advertising. Third-party services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

04

Local Data Storage

All notes, favorites, flashcards, and learning history are stored locally on your device. This data remains fully under your control and is not transmitted to our servers. You may delete this data at any time by removing the app or clearing app data.

05

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases. All payment transactions are securely handled by Apple’s App Store. We do not collect or have access to payment card details or billing information.

06

Children’s Privacy

DictX is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal data from children. The App complies with applicable children’s privacy regulations, including COPPA.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted here with a revised effective date.

08

Contact Us
